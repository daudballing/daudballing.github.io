Bikers vs. Police

Map overview of all observed bike raids, made by the law enforcement in Copenhagen, observed by the people of Copenhagen.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

The mapping of observed bike raids is a practical example of working with Google Maps and data collected from an open group for biking enthusiasts on Facebook.

The idea was simply to gather all posts from the Facebook group within the last few months, March, April and May 2014, and pin the different post to the actual locations where the observations were made.

TOOLS USED FOR THE PROJECT

As for the map, I created a custom map with Google Maps and made three different layers for each of the months of which the data was collected.

I collected data from the this Facebook Group - "Cykelrazzia". I copied each post content into my custom map, and pinned it to each described location.

OBJECTIVE

The objective is to provide the reader with a quick overview of the most common places of bike raids. At the same time, it enables the reader to find and examine the areas of town where the law enforcements seem to appear less frequently

A FEW OBSERVATIONS

Nørrebrogade, looks like taking top prize in observations, whereas similar streets like, Vesterbrogade and Østerbrogade has a significantly less amount of observations in the same period.

Dronning Louises Bro is particularly exposed with seven occurrences.

Areas as Frederiksberg, Østerbro and Amager seem less exposed.
